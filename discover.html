Confederation Trail, Prince-Edward Island

With beautiful rolling hill scenery, quaint villages and broad bay seascapes, the Confederation Trail is Prince Edward Island's portion of the Trans Canada Trail.

Maintained by Tourism P.E.I.

Lake Superior Water Trail

In 2013, we mapped the 1,000km Trans Canada Lake Superior Water Trail between Thunder Bay’s Fisherman’s Park and Whitefish Bay’s Gros Cap Harbour.

Maintained by the Lake Superior Watershed Conservancy
