From the Alexandra Bridge, the TCT heads east along the Ottawa River to Leamy Lake then follows the Gatineau River upstream to the Leamy Creek Pathway and on to Gatineau Park.
Access fee: None
Getting to the trail: From Ottawa by bike: take the Alexandra Bridge at the corner of Sussex Drive and St Patrick and cross over the Ottawa River into Quebec. At the end of the bridge the trail turns right and heads along the river bank. See Biking in Ottawa for details. Route Verte 1 connects Gatineau and Montreal along the north side of the Ottawa River, a distance of more than 200 km.
Towns along the trail: Gatineau (pop 60,000)
Points of interest: Canadian Museum of Civilization covers more than 1,000 years of Indian and Canadian history.
